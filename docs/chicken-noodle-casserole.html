Chicken Noodle Casserole

Ingredients

  • One Pasta of your choise
  • one bag of frozen veggies of your choise
  • 300g of chicken
  • 1-3 bags of Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • butter
  • butter crackers (optional)

Cooking Instruction

Estimated Time needed to cook the recipe: 30 min Required Utensils: oven, casserole dish, pot, pan


Cooking

Cook the Pasta by following the instruction on the packaging. Cook the Chicken in the pan until it has a nice brown crust. Combine all ingredients in a casserole dish. Cover the casserole with cheese and put it in the oven at 200 degrees. Meanwhile, in a sauce pan add the couple of ounces of condensed mushroom soup, condensed chicken soup and 1 cup of sour cream; season it with salt and pepper and heat it mildly and stir it finely. Pour this mixture in casserole dish; mix the chicken and pasta to it. Then take a half a cup of butter melt it; in addition, you could mix it with crumbled buttery crackers. Heat it in the oven for 30 minutes until it heated through and browned in top.
