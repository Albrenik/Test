Red Fruit Jelly

Ingredients

  • 1kg mixed berries and fruits
  • 75g sugar
  • 1 tbsp potato starch

Cooking Instruction

Estimated Time needed to cook the recipe: 30 min Required Utensils: pot, 1 big bowl, serving bowls


Cooking

Put the fruits with sugar in a pot and simmer until it's smooth. Mix the potato starch with a little bit of the fruit liquid and pour into the fruit jelly. Boil it boil briefly, fill into a big bowl or in portions in little bowls or glasses and let it cool down.
